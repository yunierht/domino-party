# Privacy Policy for Domino Party

**Effective date:** June 17, 2026

Domino Party ("the App", "we", "us") is a domino score‑tracking app. This policy
explains what information the App handles and how. By using the App you agree to
this policy.

We keep things simple: **the App has no user accounts, asks for no email or
password, and shows no ads.**

## 1. Information we handle

**a) Data stored only on your device**
The following is saved locally on your phone and is not sent to us unless you use
the live‑sharing feature (see below):
- Your matches, teams, player names you type, scores, and round history.
- Your settings (theme and language) and the display name you optionally enter.

This data stays on your device and is removed if you delete the App.

**b) Data stored in the cloud when you share a game (optional)**
The App offers an optional "Share Game / Watch a Game" feature. **Only when you
choose to share a game** do we send that game's information to our cloud database
(Google Firebase / Cloud Firestore) so other people with the game code can follow
it live. This includes:
- The team names, player names, scores, and rounds for that game.
- A short game code, and the display name you enter when scoring or requesting
  control.
- An **anonymous identifier** automatically created for your device.

Anyone who has the game code can view that shared game while it is active. Do not
put sensitive personal information in team or player names if you plan to share a
game.

**c) Anonymous authentication**
To enable live sharing, the App uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication, which
creates a random identifier for your device. This identifier is **not** linked to
your name, email, phone number, or any personal account.

**What we do NOT collect:** we do not collect your name (beyond text you type),
email, phone number, contacts, photos, precise location, or advertising
identifiers. The App contains no third‑party advertising.

## 2. How we use the information
- To run the App's features (keeping score on your device).
- To power live game‑following when you choose to share a game.
We do not sell your information or use it for advertising.

## 3. Service providers
Cloud features are provided by **Google Firebase** (Authentication and Cloud
Firestore). Data sent for live sharing is processed and stored on Google's
servers. See Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 4. Data retention and deletion
- **On‑device data:** remove it any time by deleting the App.
- **Shared game data:** shared game records may remain in our database after a
  game ends. To request deletion of a shared game or your anonymous data, contact
  us at the email below and we will remove it.

## 5. Security
We use Google Firebase's security infrastructure and access rules to protect
data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable
measures to safeguard your information.

## 6. Children's privacy
The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under
13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect
personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal
information, contact us and we will delete it.

## 7. Your rights
Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete
your information. Because the App does not require an account, most data is under
your direct control on your device. For shared‑game or anonymous data, contact us
to make a request.

## 8. Changes to this policy
We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the "Effective date"
above and, for significant changes, provide notice in the App or store listing.
