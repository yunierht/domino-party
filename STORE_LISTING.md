## Full description
Domino Party is the easiest way to keep score for your domino games.

Built for the classic game of two teams of two, Domino Party tracks every hand
and shows exactly who's winning and how many points are left to win — with big,
beautiful score rings that fill as you race to the target.

KEEP SCORE, FAST
• Two teams, four players — name them and start
• Pick your target: 100, 150, or your own
• Tap a team to add the points they won each hand
• Score rings that fill toward the win; the leader's number beats faster near victory
• Edit or fix any round
• Full match history

PLAY TOGETHER, LIVE
• Share a game code and friends follow the score live on their own phones
• Pass the pen: a watcher can request to take over scoring — with your approval
• One tap to stop broadcasting

MAKE IT YOURS
• Themes: Dark, Cuban Board, USA Board, and Carbon
• English and Spanish

No account needed. No ads. Just score your game.
